Dogs Day

A narrative-driven adventure game in which a lonely man must help three lost dogs find their way home. You play as Baloo, a reclusive academic who, on the way home from the grocery store, makes a new friend who will lead him on a life-changing journey. Act as these dogs' guide, and, in turn, they will guide you towards growth and happiness.
