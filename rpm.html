Rear Pew Mirror | Unity, C#

A backwards first-person shooter.

About the Game

Rear Pew Mirror is a straightforward first-person shooter, with one important twist: you can only shoot directly behind yourself. To aid this, players are given a rear-view mirror instead of a scope. In the main game mode, two competing teams of up to 4 players try to claim an objective and then keep it in their control until the timer runs out. There are also power-ups which you can use to sabotage other players.

The Process

Our team of three programmers and one animator created a list of 100 game ideas for our game production class, and we decided this was the most fun idea that was also feasible. We planned our core gameplay mechanics, listed the powerups and maps we wanted, and started development. We developed our first iteration and then improved it after multiple playtests.
